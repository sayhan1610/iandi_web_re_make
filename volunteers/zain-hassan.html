He/Him

Zain Hassan

Member since Apr 1, 2023

Development Coordinator

development@istanbulandi.org.tr

Zain is from Pakistan and has been at Istanbul&I since 2023. He is currently a Psychology Undergraduate at Kadir Has University, simultaneously working as an assistant researcher. His motto is "Collaboration over competition", emphasizing the importance of collective growth and learning for the success of his team.

He is quite interested in expanding his general knowledge and learning more about new skills, languages and perspectives. He enjoys listening to rock/indie music and playing AAA games. Zain wants to create opportunities for Istanbul&I that propel it to higher levels and allow it to be a place of inclusion irrespective of caste, race or creed.
