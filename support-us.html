The COVID pandemic hit us hard. Istanbul&I- Working towards a more inclusive world for all!

We have done so much with so little; but, we know that to move Istanbul&I to the next stage as an organization, we need support for capacity building. Currently, we have one paid staff member overseeing our entire volunteer structure of 250 volunteers and dozens of daily and weekly projects. We know that by hiring a small management staff, we can focus on greater development and training that would serve the community and beneficiaries for years to come. We will invest your donations into an active staff who organize vital capacity building like safeguarding training, onboarding and offboarding processes, and monitoring and evaluation processes. This will make us even more attractive to large scale grants and funding that will help us become a sustainable organization.
